Setup
Clone the repository: git clone https://github.com/sourabhm01/Sourabh-portfolio.git
Navigate to the project directory: cd personal-portfolio
Open index.html in your preferred browser.
Usage
Feel free to explore the codebase, customize it according to your preferences, and use it as a template for your own portfolio. Update the projects section with your own projects and tweak the styling to match your personal brand.

If you make significant changes or improvements, consider contributing back by creating a pull request. I'd love to see how others customize and enhance this portfolio template!
